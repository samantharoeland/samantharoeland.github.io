Samantha Roeland Physiotherapy

Providing home visits in Toronto for over 4 years in a broad range of wellness & rehabilitation services. Rest assured, you will receive a thorough assessment during your first appointment to specialize your treatment.

  • Hands-on Manual Therapy

    Manipulation of joints and tissue in an attempt to decrease pain caused by joint dysfunction, muscle spasm, muscle tension and other structural problems.

  • Osteopathy (current study)

    Treating the whole body through hands-on manipulation to address position and movement problems to enhance the body's natural ability to heal itself. Treatments may include cranial sacral therapy, myofascial techniques, and joint manipulation.

  • Physical Rehabilitation

    Improving and restoring function, self-sufficiency and mobility as a result of disease and disability including arthritis, osteoperosis, strokes, sport injuries, and surgical interventions.
